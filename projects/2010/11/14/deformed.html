de:formed
2010

de:formed is a project created for the 120ft (36.6m) long video wall at the IAC headquarters in NYC. It was coded in OpenFrameworks and employed a multiprojector / multicomputer setup.

Inspired by the work of Ryoji Ikeda, sine waves were used as graphic generators to create the desired digital aesthetic.

de:formed is a collaborative project with Tamar Ziv
