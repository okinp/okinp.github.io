Avgo
2011

Avgo is a chair that was inspired by Henrik Thor-Larsenâ€™s Ovalia egg shaped chair. It was designed in Vectorworks and fabricated using a CNC mill. No glue or screws were used in the assembly of the chair, since the slices were press-fitted together.

Avgo was featured in Core77 and exhibited in 3rd Ward and the ITP Spring Show in 2011.

Avgo was designed and fabricated with Michael Doherty.
