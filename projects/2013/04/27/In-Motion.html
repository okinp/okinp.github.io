In Motion
2013

In Motion is an improvisational dance performance with interactive sound and visuals and is the result of a collaboration with 8th Semester students of the Orff Institute in Salzburg.

The project was done with: Thomas Wagner & Gerlinde Emsenhuber, while at the MMT department of FH Salzburg.

Some more information can be found in the project page.
